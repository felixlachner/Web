Welcome Everyone!

Welcome on this website, I will be running over the process of becoming a Web developer, programmer and designer.

Web developers design, maintain and help operate websites for clients. They use their skills to create eye catching and unique websites.

The median annual salary for web programmers averages around 90K. This salary reflects on the growth potential in the field of Web developers, which is projected above average in the next 10 years.

There are many advantages and benefits to becoming a web developer, such as using technical know-how in the workspace and collaborating in groups of teammates with different expertise.
